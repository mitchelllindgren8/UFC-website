ACHIEVEMENTS

UFC

LIGHTWEIGHT CHAMPION

UFC 205 delivered as promised showcasing the featherweight champion Conor McGregor, who lived up to the hype and beat Eddie Alvarez for the lightweight belt to become the first man in UFC history to simultaneously hold two belts. The double champ racked in 1,300,00 PPV buys - $83 million while filling Madison Square Garden. The title-fight ended with a four-punch knockout, crowning a new champion.

FEATHERWEIGHT CHAMPION

In an extraordinary fashion, Conor McGregor dispatched his opponent Jose Aldo in just 13 seconds and one punch. Not only did McGregor win his first UFC belt, he also set a new UFC record of the fastest knockout in history. This victory ended Jose Aldo's 18-fight winning streak and thus dethroned the future Brazilian Hall of Famer.

INTERIM FEATHERWEIGHT CHAMPION

Originally set for a featherweight title fight with Jose Aldo, the fight was postponed due to an injury to the champion, Aldo. Conor McGregor was then set to fight Chad Mendes for the Interim-Featherweight belt in UFC 189. In a bloody bout, McGregor ruthlessly pumbles Mendes with 3 seconds to go into the second round. McGregor added to his already stunning resume by earning his 18th win with 4 straight TKOs.

CAGE WARRIORS

LIGHTWEIGHT CHAMPION

Cage Warriors 51 ended in an absoulte brutal knockout by Conor McGregor thus earning him the Cage Warriros lightweight belt. On New Year's Eve in 2012 McGregor faced off with Ivan Buchinger, and became a two title champion simultaneously. The 24-year-old from Dublin, Ireland earned himself a UFC contract immediately after the title fight by improving his record to 12-2. At this point in time the "Notorious" Conor McGregor was born.

FEATHERWEIGHT CHAMPION

After winning two fights in a row while contracted to Cage Warriors, leading Conor McGregor to his first title fight. McGregor defeated his opponent Dave Hill via submission at 4:10 of round 2 earning him the Cage Warriors featherweight belt. This would be his first title win and his first submission win in his long career showcasing his diverse mma skills.
