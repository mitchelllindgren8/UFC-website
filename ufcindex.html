ABOUT

Conor McGregor is an Irish professional mixed martial artist fighter who is signed with the Ultimate

Fighting Championship and captured the Lightweight & Featherweight Championship Belts. Over the

course of his career, he has competed as a featherweight, lightweight, and welterweight participant.

He fought in the biggest fight in combat sports history, against Floyd Meaweather August 26th, 2017.
